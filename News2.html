Congratulations Prof. Anijo Mathew for being appointed as Dean of the Institute of Design at Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago

December 10, 2022

BITMAA and SARC, BIT Mesra is very glad to share with you all that one of our distinguished alumni Mr Anijo Mathew has been appointed as the new dean of the Institute of Design (ID), Illinois Institute of Technology, Chicago, United States. Mr Mathew completed his bachelor of architecture from Birla Institute of Technology Mesra in Ranchi, India in the 1995 -2000 batch. In addition, Mr Anijo Mathew has served as an adviser and consultant to numerous global organizations in the United States, India, Taiwan, and the United Arab Emirates. We would like to congratulate Mr Anijo Mathew for his achievement and wish him great success in the future!
